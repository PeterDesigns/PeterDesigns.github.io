MAD RIVER GLEN

4 Seasons Campaign






Mad River Glen's "Four Seasons" is an informative campaign targeted at residents and vacationers. The aim of the campaign will be to increase awareness for seasonal events and activities. Each season will feature a tailored poster and stickers. Both will incorporate items, images, and copy focused on what MRG has to offer during that season.









Current Stickers

Mad River Glen gets by on essentially one piece of advertisement, their bumper sticker. People post it on their cars and much more. It has been an effective and lasting marketing strategy. I sought to expand on this idea of minimal marketing through the "Ski It If You Can" Sticker.







Local Culture

These stickers are incredibly well known throughout the area. Even non skiers would likely recognize the classic red and white design. This recognition coupled with skiers, the younger ones at least, usually decorating the back of their cars is the springboard that launches the campaign while still keeping it inexpensive.





















New "Seasons" Sticker

The final piece to the campaign will be an entirely new sticker designed with the four seasons in mind. Something different yet still promoting the mountain. I decided to go with one red "Ski It If You Can" style redo, and then made four more seasonal only stickers. The idea behind the decorative stickers is to have people marketing for us that would not put the normal bumper sticker.

MASTERS Suits & Tailors

Branding & Identity






Masters is a brand identity, created for an imaginary startup company that will be tailoring for young professionals. As a startup, Masters will have a limited budget to work with. The company can only afford a simplistic design to cut costs on printing. To attract a younger customer base, they will need a modern brand identity that is both memorable yet simple.

















Design Guides

Establishing an identity means it is crucial to have design guides. This allows multiple people to work on/for the brand without ever meeting each other. Providing a clear layout of how colors, logos, and fonts should be handled makes the design process easier for all of those involved.
















Brand Identity

The identity of masters is a clean cut one. It carries the class that is inherently associated with fine suits and tailoring. The use of few toned back colors allows the brand to be approachable by a younger crowd, while still being a serious enough to be professional.









Multi-Use Prints

Masters is designed with a budget in mind. Under those circumstances it is important that generated work have multiple uses. In this case a simple design can be used effectively as a postcard or the front of a business card.









Sticker Tags

A sticker tag is a step forward once branding is established. Using the shape of tailors chalk the tag can be used on just about any item in the store. Making it a sticker appeals to a younger crowd whom like to promote brands, while older generations who are not interested will simply treat it as a tag.




 
 
 
 

BURGER KING

Rebranding






For a conceptual rebranding of Burger King, each location will be updated to a more spohisticated, sit down restaurant. The updated establishments will feature a lounge layout allowing the patrons to feel more comfortable and make them forget about the fast food eatery it used to be.









Rebranded Marketing

The most important part of this rebrand is clarifying to people that Burger King is completely different. The posters and billboards are created to show this new feel in easy to understand methods. We are also avoiding a slogan, the goal is to get away from the gimmicks employed
by the old brand. Keeping a slogan also made the rebrand seem more like a slight change and not a complete overhaul.














Bottle Labels

Updating the food wont be too hard. You are essentially offering new items in addition to well made versions of the old favorites. As for the sauces that so many people love, they will be made to seem more artisanal. The sauces will also be sold in stores where the labels will be used. The color palette of the labels makes them stand out on shelves. Meanwhile the imagery makes selection quick and easy.










Past Acknowledgement

Finally we can completely rebrand BK without acknowledging what it used to be. One idea could be to have the new restaurants send you home with leftovers in a packaging similar to the mockup here. A nod to the old bags but an obviously nicer, more stable container.

COMMAND P

Phone Application






Command P is a phone application that simplifies complications commonly associated with office or educational printing. Immediate help is only a moment away to solve any issue that you may run into when printing on systems you don't own or control. From finding the closest working printer to getting professional help at the press of a button Command P will get the job done.












Orginal Project

Command P originally started out as a class project in which our only direction was to solve a problem. Here is the original presentation which contains early logos and names for the app that would become the Command P App.







Final Application

The final outcome was designed to limit the App's "depth" keeping it simple to use.

Report
By selecting from a list or a QR code on the printer.

Status
See what the problem is and who is fixing it.

Locate
Use the map to find the closest working printer.

Profile
Users can sign in for 2 way accountability.






BURLINGTON COLLEGE

Internship





Burlington College was a private liberal arts college located in Burlington, Vermont that offered associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees, as well as multiple professional certificate programs. An internship there included working with several professionals on in house items from/for all departments and some advertising work. Interning At Burlington College

The design work I did at Burlington College is far outweighed by what I learned there. Working closely with the head of the design department allowed me to see what is required of an in house designer. Deadlines, dealing with print shops, and making quick fixes were challenges I faced daily. These sandwich board posters are just one of a few things I did at Burlington College. Designs utilizing the logo were typical projects at Burlington college.





Accecpted Student Forms

One of the projects I am most proud of was getting their application forms to have a system. Originally students would receive close to a dozen forms when first applying, and they all looked different. I was tasked with making a layout that worked with all the forms. The consistency of the new forms makes them easier to read and complete, all while being more legitimate looking than the pervious, varied forms.














Motion Graphic

The animation largely plays off the "SPARK" Logo (Not my creation) and the motto of "Start a Fire" by mimicking a firework.
